Lakkampelly Dinesh

Founder & CEO

Lakkampelly Dinesh is a seasoned business professional and the visionary founder of Dhanya Global Exports. Drawing on a robust academic background in Business Administration and a distinguished corporate career at global leaders such as Genpact and Wipro, he brings a disciplined, process-driven methodology to the international trade sector.

After gaining invaluable international exposure in the United Kingdom and honing his expertise in procurement and operations, Dinesh returned to his roots to transform the agricultural industry.

Under his leadership, Dhanya Global Exports specializes in the strategic sourcing and global distribution of premium grains, with a core focus on Basmati rice, Non-Basmati rice, and Maize. Dinesh is dedicated to bridging the gap between local producers and world markets, ensuring every shipment adheres to the highest standards of excellence and regulatory compliance.
